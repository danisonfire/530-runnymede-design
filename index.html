Toronto, Ontario

Moody Heritage Modern

Color Specification & Design Guide

Seven Benjamin Moore paint colors, two statement wallpapers, and brass hardware throughout transform 530 Runnymede from a bright 2018 renovation into a home with depth, personality, and soul. Every bold room is balanced by calm White Dove hallways and trim — the thread that ties it all together and prevents the boldness from becoming chaos.
